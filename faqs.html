Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an email with a tracking number and link.

Q2: What is your return policy?

You can return items within 14 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unworn and in original packaging.

Q3: How long does shipping take?

Standard UK shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express options are available at checkout.

Q4: Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or cancelled within 1 hour of placing them by contacting our support team.

Q5: Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we currently ship to over 20 countries worldwide. International shipping rates apply.

Q6: How do I contact customer service?

Use the 'Contact Us' page or email us at support@voguevault.com. We're available Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm.
